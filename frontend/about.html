How It Works Features

What TwinTalk Does

TwinTalk creates intelligent, real-time AI avatars that can attend meetings on your behalf, mimic your speech patterns and expressions, take comprehensive notes, and produce detailed meeting summaries. Our technology respects user privacy while enhancing meeting productivity.

How TwinTalk Works

Avatar Creation

Create your AI avatar with a simple photo or short video. Our system generates a lightweight 3D model optimized for browser performance.

Meeting Integration

Your avatar joins meetings via secure tokens, streaming synthesized audio and animations instead of raw video for low bandwidth usage.

Smart Note-Taking

Our AI listens, understands context, and captures key information, action items, and decisions during the meeting.

Post-Meeting Summary

Receive intelligent summaries with highlights, action items, and speaker timestamps after each meeting.

Privacy & Ethics

TwinTalk is designed with privacy as a core principle. Avatar generation happens client-side when possible, recordings are strictly opt-in, and all automated actions must conform to owner-defined policies. We strongly recommend obtaining clear consent from all meeting participants before using an AI avatar.
