Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TwinTalk

How does the AI avatar feature work?

Our AI avatar can attend meetings on your behalf by analyzing your speech patterns and meeting history. It can actively participate, take notes, and provide summaries based on your preferences and past behavior.

Is my meeting data secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and follow strict security protocols to ensure all your meeting data, recordings, and notes are protected. We comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

Can I integrate TwinTalk with my calendar?

Absolutely! TwinTalk integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar, Outlook, and other popular calendar applications. You can schedule meetings directly from your calendar and our AI will automatically join at the scheduled time.

What languages does TwinTalk support?

Currently, TwinTalk supports English, Spanish, French, German, and Japanese. We're continuously working to add more languages to better serve our global user base.
